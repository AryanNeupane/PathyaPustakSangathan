About:

"Pathya Pustak Sangathan," founded by Aryan Neupane as a boys' messenger group for sharing academic resources, has undergone a profound transformation, evolving into a cohesive gang that transcends conventional academic pursuits. Initially centered around collaborative learning and sharing study materials, the group has organically expanded its scope. Beyond academic excellence, members engage in diverse activities, from organizing study sessions to participating in extracurricular events, reflecting a multifaceted identity. The once academically-focused group has cultivated a fearless ethos, empowering its members to confront challenges both within and beyond the educational realm. This evolution showcases the power of collective endeavor and the ability of digital platforms to foster holistic growth among students, turning "Pathya Pustak Sangathan" into a symbol of unity, fearlessness, and comprehensive development.
